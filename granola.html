OUR GRANOLA.

We believe in using high quality and real ingredients in our granola. Our base includes whole rolled oats, raw pumpkin seeds, raw sliced almonds, coconut chips, local honey, light brown sugar, and grass-fed butter. That's it!

UNIQUE FLAVORS.

We've devoted hours to crafting the perfect recipe that's crunchy, sweet, and salty – just the right amounts to make an addicting granola. With so many other kinds of granola on the market, we differentiate our product with our unique flavors, such as rosemary and cheddar. We encourage you to try our sweet and savory granola not just as a topping for yogurt, but on soups and salads. We also use grass-fed butter instead of refined oils.

WHY BUTTER?

We love butter.

Butter is a flavorful fat that is far superior than refined oils. Once believed to be harmful to the body, butter has now been shown to have the highest source of CLA, which inhibits cancer! Not only that, but the fatty acids in butter allow absorption of vitamins and elimination of toxins from the body.

Of course, we always believe mindful eating is key, so we like to use butter in moderation and use grass-fed, because really - grass-fed butter is not only better for you and for cows, but better in taste.
