Rosemary & Zante Currants

You'll be surprised to find how versatile your favorite herb is. Who knew it would pair so well with a sweet handful of toasted oats and nuts? We handpick fresh rosemary and throw in some dried currants to give you an aromatic granola that goes perfectly with both your yogurt and a soup or salad. Sweet, savory, and quite addicting.

Ingredients: whole rolled oats*, sliced almonds, local honey, grass-fed brown butter*, unsweetened coconut chips, light brown sugar*, pumpkin seeds*, dried unsweetened Zante currants*, rosemary*, kosher salt

*organic
